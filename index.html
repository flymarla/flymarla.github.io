Get to Know Me

As an Industrial Engineer, I focused on Lean Manufacturing and Process Improvements. I had the opportunity to work with a variety of products including mailboxes, helicopter helmets, and nuclear control valves. I decided to become a software developer after googling “I don’t want to be an engineer anymore.” Google steered me well, because after researching software development, the characteristics needed fit me incredibly well. The agile methodology of software development is very similar to lean manufacturing. Learning to code has been a very rewarding decision. I have enjoyed all the material presented and am excited for what comes next.

When not learning programming languages, I enjoy cooking, reading and hiking. I have been married for 25 years and have 5 children and 6 grandchildren. My husband and I are digital nomads, traveling the country in our motorhome with our two cats. I love YouTube and prefer it to any other form of television. My favorite food is the one I haven’t tried yet, and my favorite decision is the one I haven’t made yet.
